# Research Disclosure and Blackout Policy

## Rating Change Publication
Before publishing a rating change report, the analyst must complete conflict disclosure, confirm the issuer is not in a blackout period, and obtain supervisory review.

## Personal Trading Restrictions
Analysts may not trade covered securities within the blackout window defined by compliance. Exceptions require pre-clearance.

## Disclosure Recordkeeping
Each published report must retain disclosure language, approval timestamp, and the final recommendation rationale in the research archive.
