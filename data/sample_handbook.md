# Employee Handbook

## Onboarding Checklist
New employees must finish security awareness training, manager approval, and account provisioning review before receiving production access.
Engineering laptops must be enrolled in device compliance before privileged tools are enabled.

## Incident Workflow
Acknowledge the alert and classify severity within fifteen minutes.
Open the incident channel, assign an incident commander, and document the current customer impact.
Escalate to security if customer data exposure is suspected.

## Knowledge Contribution
Every team must publish updated runbooks after a major operational change.
Critical SOP updates must be reviewed by the domain owner before publication.
