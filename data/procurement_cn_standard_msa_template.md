# 标准主服务协议模板

## Liability Cap
Supplier's aggregate liability shall not exceed twelve months of fees paid under this agreement, except for confidentiality breach, data protection violation, and gross negligence.

## Audit Rights
Customer may conduct one audit per contract year upon reasonable notice where supplier processes sensitive customer data.

## Data Processing
Supplier shall process customer data only on documented instructions and shall not transfer customer data across borders without prior written approval.

## Security Incident Notification
Supplier must notify customer of a confirmed security incident within 24 hours.

## Termination for Convenience
Customer may terminate the agreement for convenience with 30 days written notice.
