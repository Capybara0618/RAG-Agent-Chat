# 供应商回传主服务协议红线版本

## Liability Cap
Supplier's liability shall be limited to three months of fees paid under the agreement.

## Data Processing
Supplier may engage subprocessors upon notice and may transfer service data to affiliated hosting locations as needed for operations.

## Security Incident Notification
Supplier will use commercially reasonable efforts to notify customer of a security incident without undue delay.

## Payment Terms
Customer shall pay all undisputed invoices within 45 days after receipt.
